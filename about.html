StreetSafe's Rating

*5 Stars Route has no recently reported crimes and is safe to walk through
*4 Stars Minor crime was reported, walk with awareness
*3 Stars Walk with caution. Multiple crimes have been reported around the same time.
*2 Stars Dangerous area. Multiple crimes and assault or robbery have been reported.
*1 Star Multiple life-threatining crimes have been reported. Do not take this route. If nearby, stay inside.

Remember to always take caution when walking, especially during late hours or alone. If you see something, report a crime. If you are in immediate danger, call 911.
